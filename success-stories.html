⭐ Success Stories

Real pets reunited with their families — proof that communities make a difference!

Bella the Labrador

Went missing: March 3, 2026 · Found: March 5, 2026

Where found: Under a porch two streets away from home.

Common spots to check: Porches, sheds, and under decks nearby — dogs often hide close to home when scared.

"We found Bella hiding under a porch two streets away thanks to a neighbour who saw our post. Thank you Paw Finder!"

Max the Golden Retriever

Went missing: February 14, 2026 · Found: February 16, 2026

Where found: At the local park, picked up by a family who spotted the Paw Finder notice.

Common spots to check: Parks, dog runs, and anywhere with water — retrievers love to wander toward ponds or streams.

"Max was at the park the whole time! A family recognized him from the post and called us immediately."

Luna the Black Cat

Went missing: January 20, 2026 · Found: February 1, 2026

Where found: In a neighbour's garage 4 houses down — she had snuck in and got locked inside.

Common spots to check: Garages, basements, and garden sheds — cats squeeze into tight dark spaces and can get trapped.

"We almost gave up after 10 days. Then our neighbour saw the Paw Finder post and checked their garage. Luna was safe!"

Buddy the Beagle

Went missing: December 5, 2025 · Found: December 6, 2025

Where found: Two blocks away following a smell trail into someone's backyard.

Common spots to check: Follow your nose — beagles track scents and often end up in alleys or backyards nearby.

"Buddy was found in less than 24 hours because our neighbour had seen the post that morning. So grateful!"
