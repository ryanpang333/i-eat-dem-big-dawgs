What To Do When Your Pet Goes Missing

Follow these 10 steps right away — every minute counts!

Step 1 — Search your home first Check under beds, in closets, behind furniture, and in the backyard before going anywhere else.
Step 2 — Tell your family right away More people searching at the same time means better chances of finding your pet fast.
Step 3 — Post on this website immediately Go to Post a Lost Pet and fill in the form right now — don't wait.
Step 4 — Knock on 10 neighbours' doors Show them a photo on your phone and ask if they've seen your pet today.
Step 5 — Call your local animal shelter Give them your pet's description, name, and your phone number so they can call you if the pet is brought in.
Step 6 — Put up 10 flyers in your neighbourhood Each flyer must have a clear photo, your pet's name, and your phone number. Put them on poles and in shop windows.
Step 7 — Check the shelter every 2 days Animals are dropped off all the time — don't just call once, visit in person regularly.
Step 8 — Check this website daily Someone nearby may have posted your pet on the Browse page under Found Pets.
Step 9 — Offer a reward A reward motivates people in your community to keep looking and to contact you if they spot your pet.
Step 10 — Never give up Some pets are found weeks or even months later. Keep posting, keep searching, and keep believing!
