About Paw Finder

The story behind the site.

Who Made It

Ryan built this site as a Grade 5 class project.

Why I Built It

Because I love animals and know how scary it is to lose a pet. I wanted to build something that actually helps families when they need it most.

What This Site Does

Paw Finder is a simple place where families can post a lost pet notice so people nearby can help find it. Anyone who finds a pet can also post a notice so the owner can track them down.

Who It's For

Families and neighbours in our community — kids and parents who want to help bring pets home.

Fun Fact

Millions of pets go missing every year — but most are found when communities work together. You could be the person who helps reunite a family today!

A Message to Visitors

"Together we can bring pets home!"
